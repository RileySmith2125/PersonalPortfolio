Plainledger is what happens when you decide your accounting should outlive any app. Everything is plain text — human-readable, diff-able, and yours. The CLI parses it, validates that the books balance, and gets out of the way.

On top of the ledger sits a small query language for slicing your money by account, tag, or time, plus budgets that warn before you overspend rather than after.
